About me

Hello, I'm Emil! I am a software developer aspiring to create a videogame. I like being fully involved in whichever project I'm working on in order to take informed decisions and make feasible plans.

The biggest project I've participated in was for Continental, where we were looking to develop algorithms for object detection in the path of a vehicle using a LiDAR sensor for autonomous driving. Here I worked as a KPI analyst, algorithms engineer, and even as Scrum Master for my team.

Currently, I'm working on creating a video game. For this, I learned Unity and programming in C#, which has gone really great! I'm also learning about musical composition and I have never felt as comfortable with my guitar as I do now. Additionally, I started my web dev path to gather and share my projects and things I have learned along the way.

Outside of work, I have been getting to know the world with my wonderful partner, who has supported me in all my endeavors. Also, in my free time, I like playing videogames, reading, building fun stuff on Python, playing my guitar, and hanging out with friends.
